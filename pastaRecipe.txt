Pasta Salad Recipe

1 cup rigitoni pasta
1/2 cup red bell pepper
1/2 cup brocolli
1/4 cup onion
1/2 cup italian dressing

Step 1 - boil water and when water is boiling add pasta and cook for 10 minutes

Step 2 - chop the vegetables while the pasta is cooking

Step 3 - drain excess water and rinse pasta under cold water

Step 4 - Mix ingredients together and refrigerate
